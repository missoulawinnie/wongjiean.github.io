Winner for Gamuda Inspiration Award and Star Golden Hearts Award 2023

Published on: 15/11/2023

In a triumphant moment for wildlife conservation, Bornean Sun Bear Conservation Centre (BSBCC) has been crowned the winner of both the Star Golden Hearts Award and the Gamuda Inspiration Award for 2023. The prestigious accolades were presented in recognition of BSBCC's unwavering commitment to the preservation of the endangered Bornean sun bear species and its outstanding contributions to environmental awareness.


The Star Golden Hearts Award, an initiative by The Star, Malaysia's leading English daily, aims to celebrate individuals and organizations that have made significant strides in addressing social and environmental issues. BSBCC's remarkable efforts to protect and rehabilitate Bornean sun bears, coupled with their community engagement initiatives, have earned them this esteemed recognition.


Adding to the honor, the Gamuda Inspiration Award acknowledges BSBCC's inspirational work in environmental conservation, making them stand out among other noteworthy contenders. Gamuda Berhad, a leading engineering and construction company, sponsors this award to encourage and shine a spotlight on initiatives that inspire positive change.


The recognition through these dual awards not only celebrates the accomplishments of the Bornean Sun Bear Conservation Centre but also serves as a rallying cry for continued support for the protection of endangered species and the conservation of our planet's biodiversity.

Join our Annual Charity Event!

Published on: 11/11/2023

This year’s “Adopt a Sun Bear” charity event will be held in collaboration with Crack Inc. Coffee Roasters and The LUMA Hotel - A Member of Design Hotels at the LUMA Hotel in Kota Kinabalu on 17-19th of November, 2023 from 10am to 6pm. The theme for this year is “Connect, Conserve, Collaborate”.


We will have limited merch from BSBCC as well as coffee from Crack Inc. Coffee Roasters. For those who would like to co-adopt a sun bear, the adoption can be done at the booth! A portion of all purchases and donations made will be donated back to BSBCC.


Everyone is more than welcome to stop by our booth and support our sun bears. Please help us spread the word and spread the love to our majestic sun bears!
