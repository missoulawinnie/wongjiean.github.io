Donation

Save the Sun Bears

To help give sun bears a better future, BSBCC relies on the generosity of the public. The donation funds will help us to:

  • Rehabilitate all of our rescued Sun Bears
  • Release our successfully rehabilitated Sun Bears back to the wild
  • Improve and upgrade facilities for the rescued Sun Bears
  • Build interactive education materials within the conservation centre
  • Develop more educational and awareness programmes for schools and local communities
